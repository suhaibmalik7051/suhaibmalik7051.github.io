Privacy Policy

Ye Privacy Policy batati hai ki BCgameReality par aapka basic data kaise handle hota hai. Yeh site ek awareness project hai, koi banking ya transaction platform nahi.

Information We Collect

BCgameReality khud se aapse koi sensitive personal information collect nahi karta (jaise Aadhaar, PAN, bank details, etc.).

Normal web traffic tools (jaise browser logs ya basic analytics, agar future me use kiye gaye) anonymous information store kar sakte hain, jaise:

  • Approximate location (country / city level)
  • Browser type aur device type
  • Kaunse pages zyada open kiye gaye

Ye sab sirf website ko better banane ke liye hota hai, na ki kisi user ko personally track karne ke liye.

Emails / Personal Stories

Agar aap awarenessbc@gmail.com par khud se email bhejte hain, to usme jo bhi information aap likhte hain, woh aapke control me hoti hai.

Aapki story ya details kisi public jagah par share nahi ki jaayegi jab tak aap khud clearly allow na karein. Lekin please:

  • Bank details, password ya OTP jaisi cheezen kabhi email me mat bhejein,
  • Sirf utna hi share karein jitna aapko comfortable lage.

Third-Party Links / Future Ads

Future me agar is site par ads ya external links aaye, to unka content unke apne rules ke according hoga.

BCgameReality kisi third-party website, ad ya link ke content ke liye responsible nahi hai. Aap jab bhi kisi external site par jaate hain, please unki apni Privacy Policy aur Terms zaroor padhein.

Changes to This Policy

Time ke saath, is Privacy Policy me changes ho sakte hain. Jab bhi major change hoga, yahi page update kiya jaayega.
