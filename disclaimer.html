Not The Official BC Game Website

Ye website sirf awareness ke liye banayi gayi hai.

No Gambling Promotion

Is website par kahi bhi:

  • Real money gambling ka promotion nahi hota,
  • “Bet lagao”, “yahan khelo” jaisi koi advice nahi di jaati,
  • Kisi bhi gambling platform ka sponsored content nahi hota.

Yahan jo bhi likha hai, woh sirf gambling ke nuksaan aur addiction ke risk samjhane ke liye hota hai.

Not Financial, Legal or Medical Advice

Is website ka content:

  • Kisi bhi tarah ki financial advice nahi hai,
  • Kisi legal advice ka replacement nahi hai,
  • Aur na hi kisi doctor ya therapist ki medical advice hai.

Agar aap serious financial, legal ya mental health problem face kar rahe ho, toh kisi qualified professional se direct help lena sabse sahi rahega.
